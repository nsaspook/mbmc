Controller Commands

Selecting the option and clicking the Send button 
will send the command to the Solar Controller.
Use only one option at a time.

Pressing the Reset buttion will clear all Selections.

The Utility Charger is ~mbmcdata(200)~.

Force Charger to Next battery.
Turn On Utility powered Charger.
Turn Off Utility powered Charger.
Set SOC to 100% on Battery being Charged.
